San Diego 250 ATPHard

The 2022 San Diego Open was a men's tennis tournament played on outdoor hard courts. It was the 2nd edition of the San Diego Open, and part of the ATP Tour 250 series of the 2022 ATP Tour. It takes place two weeks after US Open, from 19.09.2022 to 25.09.2022. Among players who will play are Daniel Evans, Jenson Brooksby, Pedro Martinez, Marcos Giron, Jiri Vesely, Brandon Nakashima... After beating Dimitrov in the Semi Final Casper Ruud went on to win the final of the 2021 San Diego Open beating Cameron Norrie 6-0 6-2 and was handed the trophy by Rod Laver. It was Ruud's fifth victory of the 2021 Season.

Budapest Open 125WTAClay

The 2022 Budapest Open is an upcoming professional tennis tournament to be played on outdoor clay courts. It will be the fourth edition of the tournament and first as a WTA 125 which is also part of the 2022 WTA 125 tournaments, offering a total of $115,000 in prize money. It will take place at the Római Teniszakadémia in Budapest, Hungary between 19 and 25 September 2022. Players who will play are Begu Irina with Wild Card, Bondar Anna, Paolini Jasmine, Bronzetti Lucia, Parrizas Diaz Nuria... 2018 winner was Viktoria Kuzmova.

Moselle Open 250 ATPIndoorHard

Metz is a city located at the confluence of the Moselle and Seille rivers. The 2022 Moselle Open is a tennis tournament played on indoor hard courts. It is the 24rd edition of the Moselle Open, and part of the ATP World Tour 250 Series of the 2022 ATP Tour. Players who will play are Danill Medvedev, Dominic Thiem,Huber Hurkacz, Carreno Busta, Gael Monfils, Lorenzo Musetti, Holger Rune, Karen Khachanov. Defending champion is Hubert Hurkacz who beated Carreno Busta in two sets 7:6,6:3.

Korea Open 250WTAHard

The 2022 Korea Open (also known as the 2022 Hana Bank Korea Open for sponsorship purposes) is a combined ATP Tour and WTA Tour tennis tournament and takes place in Seoul, South Korea at the Olympic Park Tennis Center on hard courts from 19 to 25 September 2022 for the women and from 26 September to 2 October 2022 for the men.It is the 18th edition of the women's tournament, and the first edition of the men's tournament since 1996. The tournament is a WTA 250 event on the 2022 WTA Tour (after being a WTA 125 level tournament in 2021), and a ATP Tour 250 event on the 2022 ATP .

Torray Pan Pacific Open 500WTAIndoorHard

In the capital of Japan,Tokyo Toray Pan Pacific 2022 Open is professional women's tennis tournament played on indoor hard courts. It will be the 37th edition of the Pan Pacific Open, and part of the WTA 500 tournaments of the 2022 WTA Tour. It will take place at the Ariake Coliseum in Tokyo, Japan, on 19–25 September 2022. Paula Badosa, Caroline Garcia, Garbine Muguruza,Veronika Kudermetova, Beatriz Haddad Maia and others will participate. Naomi Osaka has won last tournament in 2019. She beated Pavlyuchenkova in two sets 6:2,6:3.
